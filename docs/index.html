Terms & Privacy Policy

1. Data We Process

When you register, we store your email address and display profile details (name, profile photo) to identify you to other users. Chat messages are stored in encrypted format in our secure cloud database and can only be decrypted by the intended recipient's private key.

2. Message Security & Cryptography

All messaging uses full client-side encryption. Plain-text message content is never transmitted to, nor stored on, our servers. Encryption keys are negotiated peer-to-peer between sender and recipient devices.

3. Disappearing Content & Third-Party Storage

Story media uploads are temporarily hosted using secure, anonymous file-hosting services and are programmatically deleted after 24 hours. No permanent logs, backups, or copies of story content are retained by us or any third party.

4. Your Rights & Data Control

You retain full ownership and control of your account, messages, and local key store at all times. You can export a backup of your personal data or permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time directly through the in-app account settings.
